Pizza Margherita Recipe

Description

Pizza margherita is a classic dish that is enjoyed around the world. It is simple, but delicious if made correctly.

Here you will find an easy pizza margherita recipe that will leave you craving more with each slice.

Ingredients

  • 1 12-inch round of pizza dough, stretched
  • 3 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 ounces fresh mozzarella
  • 4 to 5 basil leaves, roughly torn

Steps

  1. Place a pizza stone or tiles on the middle rack of your oven and turn the heat to its highest setting. Let it heat for at least an hour.
  2. Put the sauce in the center of the stretched dough and use the back of a spoon to spread it evenly across the surface, stopping approximately Â½ inch from the edges.
  3. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pie. Break the cheese into large pieces and place these gently on the sauce. Scatter basil leaves over the top.
  4. Using a pizza peel, pick up the pie and slide it onto the heated stone or tiles in the oven. Bake until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling, approximately 4 to 8 minutes.

Enjoy your pizza!
