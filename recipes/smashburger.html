Smash Burger Recipe

Description

Smash burgers are made by pressing a beef patty onto a piping hot griddle, creating a crispy, carmelized crust with a juicy interior.

It's simple, fast, and packed with flavor. Here is my version of a smash burger!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 brioche burger buns
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)
  • Sliced onions
  • Your favorite sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions.
  3. Place one portion on the hot skillet and smash it down with a spatula to form a thin patty.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the edges are crispy, then flip and cook another 1-2 minutes.
  6. Toast the burger buns in butter until golden.
  7. Assemble the burgers with the patty, sliced onions, and your favorite sauce.

Enjoy your smash burger!
